PIZZA PLACE THANKS YOU


THE FOOD

The world's #1 pizza brand is now serving in Nairobi City.

One of Nairobi’s most celebrated pizzerias, PIZZA PLACE combines the elegance of a Bolognese enoteca with the fun bustle of an African society. Open daily from 11AM to midnight, Pizza Place is perfect for a quick snack, a lingering meal, a family night out, or a large social gathering.

The first restaurant in Nairobi City to have a Cured Meats HACCP plan approved by the Nairobi City Health Department, Pizza Place is renowned for its extensive selection of house-cured meats. The antipasti strike “the winning simplicity that defines Italian food at its best,” says William Grimes in his New York Times review.

But what sets Pizza Place apart from its peers are its dolci, namely the widely sought after gelato. Like the savory menu, gelato flavors include the traditional– dark chocolate, olive oil– as well as the seasonal.
